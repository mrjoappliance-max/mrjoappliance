Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you repair my appliance?

+

We offer same-day service for most appliance repairs in Baton Rouge! When you call, we'll schedule a convenient time, often within hours. Our technicians arrive with fully stocked trucks, so most repairs are completed on the first visit.

What brands of appliances do you service?

+

We repair all major appliance brands including Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, Maytag, Frigidaire, KitchenAid, Bosch, Electrolux, and many more. Our technicians are factory-trained and equipped to handle any brand or model.

Do you charge for diagnosis?

+

We charge a service call fee for diagnosis, which is waived if you proceed with the repair. You'll receive upfront pricing before any work begins - no hidden fees or surprises!

Is your work guaranteed?

+

Absolutely! We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. All repairs come with a warranty on parts and labor. If you experience any issues, we'll make it right at no additional cost.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and checks. Payment is due upon completion of the repair. We make it easy and convenient for our Baton Rouge customers.

Should I repair or replace my appliance?

+

Our honest technicians will assess your appliance and provide transparent advice. Generally, if the repair cost is less than half the replacement cost and your appliance is under 10 years old, repair is the smart choice. We'll help you make the best decision for your situation and budget.

Do you offer emergency appliance repair?

+

Yes! We understand that appliance emergencies don't follow a schedule. We offer emergency repair services 7 days a week in Baton Rouge and surrounding areas. Call us anytime at (225) 555-REPAIR for urgent repairs.
